# 🎯 Angle of Attack

**A two-player artillery duel that teaches geometry.**

Take turns blasting each other off a random mountain. Set your angle and speed, read the wind, and figure out the right trajectory — no auto-aim, no hand-holding. Every shot is a geometry lesson.

## How to Play

1. **Random mountain** is generated every round
2. **P1 (blue)** on the left, **P2 (red)** on the right
3. Set your **Angle** (↑↓) and **Speed** (←→)
4. Press **Fire** (Enter/Space)
5. Watch your cannonball arc through the air
6. First direct hit wins! Each player has 3 lives

## Controls

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| ↑ / ↓ | Adjust angle |
| ← / → | Adjust speed |
| Enter / Space | Fire! |
| 📐 Geometry button | Shot analysis panel |

## Why It Teaches Geometry

- **45°** gives maximum range — see for yourself
- **Uphill targets** need a steeper angle, **downhill** needs shallower
- **Wind** pushes your shot — learn to compensate
- The **Geometry panel** shows you your flight time, max height, flat range, and distance to target
- Every miss is feedback — dial in your aim
